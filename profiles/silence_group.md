Silence Group

**Aliases**  
- **Silence** (Group-IB naming).  
- Potential overlap with **TA505** or other financially motivated groups.

**Overview**  
- **Motivation**: Large-scale financial theft from banks, focusing on Russian and Eastern European institutions, though Western banks have also been targeted.  
- **Targets**: Banks, ATM networks, e-payment systems.  
- **Geography**: Initially in post-Soviet countries, expanding globally.

### TTPs
1. **Phishing**
   - Weaponized attachments with elaborate social engineering.

2. **Malware Toolkits**
   - **Silence trojan** for reconnaissance, credential harvesting, ATM control.

3. **Attack Lifecycle**
   - Reconnaissance on bank internal systems, culminating in ATM cash-outs or unauthorized fund transfers.

### Last Known Activity
- **Sporadic** high-value hits reported into 2022.  
- Continues to refine phishing lures and trojans.

### Tools & Infrastructure
- **Silence Trojan**: Custom code with remote access, keylogging, and audio/video capture.  
- **Infrastructure**: Frequent domain rotation, bulletproof hosting.
