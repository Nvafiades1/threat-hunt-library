Lazarus Group (APT38 Sub-group)

**Aliases**  
- Part of the North Korean Lazarus umbrella, specifically **APT38** for financially focused operations.  
- Also referred to as **Bluenoroff** in certain contexts.

**Overview**  
- **Motivation**: State-sponsored financially motivated attacks to fund the DPRK regime.  
- **Targets**: Global banking organizations, cryptocurrency exchanges, and financial institutions.  
- **Geography**: Worldwide, with a focus on SWIFT systems and crypto platforms.

### TTPs
1. **Spear Phishing**
   - Leveraging socially engineered lures targeting bank employees (HR recruitment lures, business proposals).

2. **Bank Heists**
   - Known for the **Bangladesh Bank** SWIFT heist in 2016 (~$81 million stolen).

3. **SWIFT Manipulation**
   - Specialized TTPs for bypassing bank transaction verifications.

4. **Cryptocurrency Exchange Attacks**
   - High-profile crypto thefts (e.g., Axie Infinity’s Ronin Bridge hack in 2022).

5. **Sophisticated Evasion**
   - Use of custom malware families (e.g., **AppleJeus**, **COPPERHEDGE**, **FASTCash**).

### Last Known Activity
- **Ongoing**: Continues to target banks, fintech, and crypto for large-scale theft.  
- Publicly tied to 2022–2023 cryptocurrency heists totaling hundreds of millions.

### Tools & Infrastructure
- **SWIFT-focused malware**: Tailored trojans to manipulate bank transactions.  
- **Crypto-Exploiting Tools**: Tools to manipulate or compromise cryptocurrency wallets and exchanges.
