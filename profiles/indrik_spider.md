Evil Corp (Indrik Spider)

**Aliases**  
- **Evil Corp**, **Indrik Spider**, **Dridex** Group, **TA505**.  

**Overview**  
- **Motivation**: Massive financial gains through banking Trojans, ransomware, and large-scale phishing.  
- **Targets**: Banks, financial institutions, large enterprises with valuable data.  
- **Geography**: Global, with a focus on North America and Europe.

### TTPs
1. **Initial Access**
   - Malspam campaigns distributing **Dridex** or similar banking malware.  
   - Exploit macros in malicious documents.

2. **Banking Malware**
   - **Dridex**: Capable of web injection to steal online banking credentials.

3. **Ransomware**
   - **BitPaymer**, **WastedLocker**, **Hades** – used for large-scale extortion.

4. **Adaptation to Sanctions**
   - Constant rebranding of ransomware families to circumvent sanctions placed on Evil Corp by U.S. Treasury.

### Last Known Activity
- **Persistent** Dridex-based campaigns through 2023, with multiple rebrands of their ransomware to avoid detection and sanctions.

### Tools & Infrastructure
- **Malspam Botnets**: High-volume spam distribution.  
- **Fast-flux / Bulletproof Hosting**: For Dridex C2.
