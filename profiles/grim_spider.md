Grim Spider

### Aliases / Other Names
- Often tied directly to **Ryuk Ransomware** operations.

### Known Targets / Victims
- **Healthcare, Public Sector, and Critical Infrastructure**.
- Major victims historically located in the U.S. and parts of Europe.

### Common TTPs
1. **Initial Access**
   - Malware-laced email attachments or embedded links leading to loaders (e.g., TrickBot).

2. **Malware & Ransomware**
   - **Ryuk**: High-profile ransomware used to encrypt critical systems.

3. **Privilege Escalation & Lateral Movement**
   - Use of stolen domain admin credentials, existing corporate tools, and batch scripts.

4. **Extortion**
   - Data encryption with the threat of public disclosure.

### Recent Activity
- Linked to numerous high-profile Ryuk attacks from 2018 into 2021.
- Current shifts in the ransomware scene make direct attribution more difficult, but techniques remain relevant.

### Tools & Infrastructure
- **Ryuk Ransomware**: Proprietary or heavily customized code.
- **TrickBot / BazarLoader**: Often used in collaboration with Wizard Spider.
