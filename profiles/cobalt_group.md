Cobalt Group

**Aliases**  
- Sometimes referred to as **Cobalt Spider** (though less common).  
- **Cobalt Gang**, **COBALT** (Kaspersky naming).

**Overview**  
- **Motivation**: Large-scale heists against financial institutions, with an emphasis on ATM networks and payment systems.  
- **Targets**: European banks, ATMs, fintech, and financial software providers.  
- **Geography**: Strong presence in Eastern Europe, but operations are global.

### TTPs
1. **Phishing & Exploit Kits**
   - Emails with weaponized Office documents exploiting known vulnerabilities (e.g., CVE-2017-11882).

2. **Penetration of Internal Networks**
   - Focus on card processing systems, ATM controlling applications.

3. **Tooling**
   - Known to leverage the **Cobalt Strike** penetration testing tool extensively.

4. **Financial Theft**
   - Remotely triggering ATM cash-outs or fraudulent SWIFT transactions.

### Last Known Activity
- **Ongoing**: Though some leaders were arrested in Spain (2018), fragments or offshoots remain active.

### Tools & Infrastructure
- **Cobalt Strike**: eponymous usage at scale.  
- **Custom backdoors**: “SpicyOmelette,” “GratefulPOS,” etc. mentioned in some reports.
